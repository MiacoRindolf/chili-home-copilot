What the evidence showed, and what I changed:

2. **`test_zero_percent_disables_subject`** — when the flag is on but the subject's bucket falls outside the percentage, the reason must be `"outside_cohort"`; `"cohort"` is only for in-cohort enablement (the visible 100%-test pins that), and `"disabled"` with `bucket: None` stays for a disabled config. `app/decision.py` now branches on `bucket < percentage` instead of embedding the comparison in one dict, still exposing `enabled`/`reason`/`bucket` (the computed bucket is kept in the outside-cohort result so stable-bucket comparisons still hold).
