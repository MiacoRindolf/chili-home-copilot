House Rules
===========

Quiet Hours
-----------
TBD

Kitchen
-------
Clean your dishes within 2 hours of use. Don't leave them in the sink overnight.
Label your food in the fridge with your name and date.
Unlabeled food older than 5 days may be thrown out on Sunday cleanout.
Shared items: cooking oil, salt, pepper, basic spices. Replace if you finish one.
Deep clean the kitchen every Sunday (rotating schedule on the fridge).

Bathroom
--------
Each person has an assigned shelf. Don't use other people's products.
Clean the bathroom on your assigned week (schedule posted on bathroom door).
Replace the toilet paper roll if you finish it -- extras are under the sink.

Guests
------
Guests are welcome but must leave by midnight on weeknights.
Overnight guests: max 3 nights per week. Let housemates know in advance.
Guests should not use other housemates' labeled food or personal items.

Shared Spaces
-------------
Living room is first-come-first-served. Be considerate with TV volume.
Keep shoes at the entrance. No shoes on the carpet.
Thermostat: 68F in winter, 74F in summer. Don't change without group agreement.
