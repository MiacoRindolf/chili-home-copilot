Household Favorite Recipes
==========================

Rindo's Garlic Fried Rice
-------------------------
Ingredients: 3 cups day-old rice, 6 cloves garlic (minced), 2 tbsp soy sauce,
1 tbsp sesame oil, 2 eggs, green onions for garnish.
Instructions: Heat sesame oil in a wok on high. Fry garlic until golden (1 min).
Add rice, break up clumps, stir-fry 3 minutes. Push rice to the side, scramble
eggs, then mix everything together. Add soy sauce, toss, and top with green
onions. Serves 2-3.

Easy Pasta Aglio e Olio
-----------------------
Ingredients: 400g spaghetti, 6 cloves garlic (sliced thin), 1/2 cup olive oil,
1 tsp red pepper flakes, fresh parsley, parmesan.
Instructions: Cook pasta al dente, reserve 1 cup pasta water. In a large pan,
heat olive oil on medium-low, add garlic and pepper flakes, cook 3 minutes
(don't let garlic brown). Add drained pasta and 1/2 cup pasta water, toss until
coated. Finish with parsley and parmesan. Serves 4.

Smoothie (Weekday Breakfast)
----------------------------
Ingredients: 1 banana, 1 cup frozen berries, 1 cup milk (or oat milk),
2 tbsp peanut butter, 1 tbsp honey.
Instructions: Blend everything for 60 seconds. Pour and go. Serves 1.
