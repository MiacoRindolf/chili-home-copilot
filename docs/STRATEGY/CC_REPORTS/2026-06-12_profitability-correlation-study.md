Method: simulated entry at the blocked moment's price (payload mid, else nearest momentum_nbbo_spread_tape tick, else 1m bar close), -1R stop at 1.2%, forward R at +5/+15/+30 min from tape mid path (equities) or 1m bars; spread gate additionally got the honest version (enter at payload ask, exit at forward tape bid). HEADLINE: essentially every gate PAID this week. Raw uncapped means look positive for a few trigger-wait gates, but that is pure lottery-tail (WCT +48R, GELS +31R, CUPR +32R single-symbol outliers) which the lane's 2:1 bracket cannot capture — capped at +2R, every gate with n>=10 is negative at +30m except waiting_for_reclaim (n=5, too thin).
