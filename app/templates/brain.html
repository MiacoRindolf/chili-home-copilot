Trading Brain

Quant operating model Research discipline for this desk — robust evidence, not one hero curve.
  • Hypothesis first — treat each pattern as testable; say what would falsify it.
  • Universe & timeframe — asset class, symbols or filters, and bar size before trusting stats.
  • In-sample vs out-of-sample — prefer OOS / hold-out when CHILI exposes it; flag when only IS exists.
  • Costs & tails — commission/spread proxies in backtests; max drawdown and streak risk matter.
  • Sample size — low trade counts are noisy; stability beats a single pretty equity line.
