CHILI-managed positions

Open positions managed by CHILI, including pattern-linked entries, AutoTrader v1 rows, and AI or manual position plans with saved stop or target levels. Per-row controls let you pause the exit monitor on a position, exclude it from future synergy scale-ins, or close it now. Desk Pause entries blocks new automated entries; it does not touch monitors. Server must have CHILI_AUTOTRADER_ENABLED=true for the scheduler tick to run.
