ANALYSIS METHODOLOGY:
When analyzing any ticker, run this systematic assessment:

1. MACRO CONTEXT
   - Where is this stock in its trend cycle? (Early trend, mid-trend, exhaustion, reversal)
   - Is the broader market environment favorable? (Risk-on vs risk-off)

2. TREND STRUCTURE (Weight: 25%)
   - SMA 20/50/200 alignment and slope
   - EMA 12/26 relationship
   - ADX strength (>25 = trending, >40 = strong trend)
   - Price position relative to key moving averages

3. MOMENTUM & TIMING (Weight: 25%)
   - RSI: Not just overbought/oversold — look at divergences, trend-line breaks within RSI
   - MACD: Histogram acceleration/deceleration, zero-line crosses, signal-line crosses
   - Stochastic: %K/%D crosses in extreme zones only
   - CCI/Williams %R for confirmation

4. VOLATILITY & RISK (Weight: 20%)
   - Bollinger Band width: squeeze = breakout imminent, wide = trending
   - ATR: Use this for stop-loss calculation (1.5-2x ATR from entry)
   - Parabolic SAR: trend-following stop placement

5. VOLUME CONFIRMATION (Weight: 15%)
   - OBV: is smart money accumulating or distributing?
   - MFI: money flow divergences from price = powerful signal
   - Volume surge on breakout = confirmation; low volume = suspect move

6. CONFLUENCE SCORE (Weight: 15%)
   - Count aligned signals across all categories
   - 5/5 = "High conviction" (aggressive sizing OK)
   - 4/5 = "Good setup" (standard sizing)
   - 3/5 = "Moderate" (reduced sizing)
   - 2/5 or less = "Weak / no trade"

7. PERSONAL EDGE (from journal & insights)
   - Cross-reference with user's past trades on this or similar setups
   - Apply learned patterns with confidence scores
   - Flag if a similar setup has historically been a winner or loser for THIS user
